# MONETIZATION PLAYBOOK

## Phase 1: Free Beta (Month 1-2)
Goal: Get 50-100 users, gather feedback
- Launch on ProductHunt, HackerNews, Reddit (r/algotrading, r/stocks)
- Offer free access in exchange for testimonials
- Build case studies with 10-20 power users

## Phase 2: Paid Launch (Month 3)
Goal: First ₹50,000/month revenue
- Convert 20% of free users to Pro (₹999/month) = 10-20 users = ₹10-20K
- Land 5 Business clients (₹4,999/month) = ₹25K
- Total: ₹35-45K/month

## Phase 3: Scale (Month 4-6)
Goal: ₹2,00,000/month revenue
- SEO + Content marketing
- Affiliate partnerships with trading platforms
- YouTube channel showing live recommendations
- 100 Pro users + 20 Business clients

## Phase 4: Exit or Scale (Month 6-12)
Option A: Sell business for ₹50-80 lakhs
Option B: Scale to ₹10L+/month, raise funding

## Customer Acquisition Costs
- Organic (blog, YouTube): ₹0
- Paid ads: ₹500-1000 per customer
- LTV: ₹12,000 (1 year retention)
- LTV/CAC ratio: 12x (excellent)

## Key Metrics to Track
- Daily Active Users
- Conversion rate (free → paid)
- Churn rate
- Average recommendation accuracy
- User feedback/NPS score
